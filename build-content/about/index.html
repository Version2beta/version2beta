About version2beta

I'm a geek and a rad dad dude[1] who likes to be outside, listen to music, camp, and hike — sometimes all at the same time. My work in tech not only keeps me happy and off the streets, it also pays for the beer and the kids.

I write (mostly here), speak on tech topics[2], and do devops[3] and coding.

Currently, I'm working as a developer at iTV[4] in Provo, UT, where I help create platforms for cultural discourse about broadcast media.
